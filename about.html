Biography

Ryan McKnight is a scuba diver, freediver and alpine mountaineer.

He founded and leads the multidisciplinary and award-winning design team at Industry Dive, a rapidly growing business news company in Washington, DC. In 2017, he was recognized as an "Emerging Leader" in B2B media for his work at Industry Dive.

Prior to arriving in DC, McKnight researched environmental governance and internet governance at The London School of Economics, completing an MSc in Global Politics in 2012. During the spring of 2011, McKnight worked for the U.S. Dept. of State at the U.S. Mission to the United Nations, preparing environmental governance briefing materials for Ambassador Susan Rice.
