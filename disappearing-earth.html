Disappearing Earth

This body of work documents parts of our planet that are rapidly changing, places and species that are disappearing. The snows of Kilimanjaro. The ice caves of Mendenhall Glacier.

My "Disappearing Earth" photos embody how, at the end of the day, we are all just chasing after time. The images were shot on a range of digital cameras – mirrorless, DSLR and fixed lens.
