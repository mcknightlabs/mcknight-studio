Artist Statement

Scarcity is the central theme of my work.

Scarcity of subject. I photograph parts of our planet that are rapidly changing, places and species that are disappearing. The snows of Kilimanjaro. The ice caves of Mendenhall Glacier. I also capture rare views of "the other earth" that most people will never see – the underwater world.

Scarcity of medium. When I take photos in urban areas for my "City Windows" gallery, I always use a scarce, impermanent medium – film.

Scarcity of prints. I only sell limited edition prints.

Self-mastery is an important part of my identity. To capture these images, I have had to spend many hours over multiple years training as a scuba diver, free diver and mountaineer.
